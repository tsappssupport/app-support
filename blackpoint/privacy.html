Blackpoint Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Blackpoint does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Photos and Files

Your photos, RAW files, edits, Recipes, presets, and exports are processed locally on your Mac and are not uploaded or transmitted by Blackpoint.

Blackpoint accesses only the files and folders that you choose to open or import.

Personal Data

Blackpoint does not require a user account and does not collect personal information.

Blackpoint does not use analytics, advertising trackers, third-party tracking SDKs, or advertising services.

In-App Purchases

Optional in-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.

Blackpoint does not receive or store your payment information. Apple's handling of purchase and account information is governed by Apple's own privacy policies and terms.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if Blackpoint's features or privacy practices change. Any updates will be published on this page.
