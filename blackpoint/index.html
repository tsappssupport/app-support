Blackpoint Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Blackpoint is designed with privacy in mind. Your photographs and edits are processed locally on your Mac.

Photos and Files

Blackpoint accesses only the photos, folders, presets, and other files that you choose to open or import.

Blackpoint does not upload your photographs to our servers. Your original files remain under your control.

Personal Data

Blackpoint does not require an account and does not collect, sell, or share personal information for advertising or tracking.

In-App Purchases

Blackpoint may offer optional purchases through Apple's App Store. Purchases are processed by Apple and are subject to Apple's privacy practices and terms.

Third-Party Services

Blackpoint does not use third-party advertising or tracking services.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as Blackpoint evolves. Any updates will be published on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, contact: ts.apps.support@gmail.com
