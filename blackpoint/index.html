Frequently Asked Questions

What photo formats does Blackpoint support?

Blackpoint supports RAW photo formats available through macOS, along with common image formats such as JPEG and PNG.

Does Blackpoint modify my original RAW files?

No. Blackpoint uses non-destructive editing. Your original photographs remain unchanged.

What are Recipes?

Recipes are reusable editing looks. You can create your own Recipes or import compatible XMP presets and LUT-based looks.

I'm having trouble with a photo or Recipe.

Email us with your Mac model, macOS version, and a description of what happened. Screenshots are helpful when possible.
