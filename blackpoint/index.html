Frequently Asked Questions

What photo formats does Blackpoint support?

Blackpoint supports RAW photo formats available through macOS, along with common image formats such as JPEG and PNG.

Does Blackpoint modify my original photos?

No. Blackpoint uses non-destructive editing. Your original photographs remain unchanged.

What are Recipes?

Recipes are reusable editing looks that can be applied to your photos. You can create your own Recipes and import compatible XMP presets and LUT-based looks.

Does Blackpoint collect my photos or personal data?

No. Blackpoint processes your photos and edits locally on your Mac and does not collect or upload your personal data.

I'm having trouble with Blackpoint.

Email us with a description of the issue. Including your Mac model, macOS version, and screenshots can help us understand the problem.
